Almond Green Salad

Fresh and Crispy

1/2pound bacon
2 tablespoons white wine vinegar\
3 tablespoons honey
1/2 teaspoons dry hot mustard
1/2 teaspoons table salt
1/2 teaspoon ground paprika
1/4 cup olive oil
1 head reed leaf lettuce torn into bite-sized pieces
1 (15 ounces) can mandarin oranges, drained
1 bunch green onions diced
3/4 cup silvered almonds


Directions 1.In medium skillet over medium-heat, cook bacon until evenly browned. Drain cool, and crumble.
2.To make the dressing, thorougly blend the vinegar, money, dry mustard, celery salt, paprika, and olive oil.
3.Place lettuce, oranges, green onions, bacon, and almonds in a serving bowl. Toss with dressing and serve.
